/ FAQS



Can I place an order via Instagram DM?
No, please place an order via our website.
Please place an order and make payment by Friday.

Am I able to reschedule or cancel my order once it is confirmed?
Nope, we do not allow cancellation or rescheduling of orders due to our limited daily capacity.
Do double check before ordering.

Can I order to multiple locations?
Yes, you can! You will have to place separate orders for delivery to different locations.

More information on delivery
We will deliver your cake(s) between 11am and 6pm.
We do not deliver to restricted zones (hospital, quarantine zone, airport, Jurong island, army camp, Tuas)
Free Delivery for orders over $110 for the same date and address.

Do you have self-collection?
Yes, we do. Our team will liaise with you when to collect.
Address: 1 Jalan Keli, Singapore 577937

Can I pay cash on delivery/self-collection?
Sorry, we do not accept cash. We only accept card payments, PayLah, PayNow and GrabPay.

How should I store my cheesecake?
Please store the cake in the fridge for 2 hours upon receiving it. Leave the cake out for 15 minutes before consuming.
Do not store it in the freezer or at room temperature. Do not heat up the cake. The cake can last up to 3 days.

How should I store my madeleines?
You can store the madeleines at room temperature or in the fridge.
Heat them up before eating!! They can last up to 2-3 days.
